About ExaMPS 2.0

ExaMPS 2.0 is the new and improved version of the original ExaMPS project. The first version made last year was not finished, so it wasn’t very useful for the school. Our group decided to continue the work and make it better so that teachers and students can really use it.


This new version has a better design, runs faster, and is easier to use. It helps teachers create exams, check scores, and record the Mean Percentage Score (MPS) in a simpler and quicker way.

Our Story

ExaMPS 2.0 was re-developed by Grade 12 – Buoyant, Group 5 for the School Year 2025–2026, under the guidance of Mrs. Janette G. De Ramos at Famy National Integrated High School. Our goal was to finish what was started last year and turn it into a complete system that the school can really use.

Last year’s system didn’t have all the needed parts and wasn’t ready for actual use. So, our team worked hard to make it complete and useful for teachers and students. This new version is more secure, faster, and now works on both computers and mobile devices. With ExaMPS 2.0, teachers can handle exams easily, and students can take tests smoothly.
